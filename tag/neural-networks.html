Coursera's Machine Learning

 Thu 31 October 2019 by Reaz H.

In November 2017, I fired up my PC and enrolled myself in Andrew Ng's very popular Machine Learning course on Coursera. He teaches this as a course at Stanford; however, the Stanford version utilizes python/numpy while the Coursera version uses Octave. In this blog post, I will link to my repository which has all the solved problems for the course. I will also attempt to explain some of the concepts in an effort to rehash topics long forgotten.
